Final Website

My final website I feel is playing with vision and giving that impression of loosing vision. I like having the multiple random dots as they remind me of when you look at a light and then close your eyes, seeing illusions of what you were looking previously. In addition, the centre circle engulfs the screen, eventuaually covering where the mouse can click. completely "blinding" the user from where there mouse is located.
