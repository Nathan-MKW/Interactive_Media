Moving Image Poster

Concepts

My concept for this poster always started with the idea of having something feel and look more retro. In concept 1 I was looking at different ways I can use popular contemporary design formats for a poster while in concept 2 I was looking at recreating both a laptop and 90's square desktop computer. in the end I merged the two concepts, the layout of concept one with the actual physical concept of concept 2. replacing the laptop with an old cellphone instead to comlpete this theme of vintage tech. the colour scheme I decided on was not my origional colour pallete, instead going for colours I found in pleanty of 2000's websites while looking online (besides blue, I did not like the blue at all).

Workthroughs

I struggled with making the poster have an aesthetically pleasing layout while trying to, not only get the three layers of text, bit also fit the work "Theorizing" into the title square. Through an in-class crit, my final layout was decided which helped me immensely. I also made the letters a bit darker than their background in the textface so they could be more legiable through the mixture of flashing and the lights.
