References work

Through all of my iterations I knew what I wanted my references page to revolve around. I wanted all the references to be attached to sheep in some way as it is a play on "finding all these references was like herding sheep" as well sheep "primarily what they produce" being a major part of my art practice. I sarted with wanted to make it so that when you hover over a sheep it would remove the rest and give you the information that sheep holds. this would be the link and some information on what you would find. after the final class, it was discussed that I should have all of your references easily retreavable hence why I ended up placing them ontop of the sheep. I feel it still has that fun aesthetic on a page I would have normally made much more boring.

Readings

I wanted the reading page to be quite simple as I know when I am reading a text I am easily distracted so having a very simple layout where any writing or imagery that may be distracting while reading a response is hidden until the mouse reveals the response to the viewer.
