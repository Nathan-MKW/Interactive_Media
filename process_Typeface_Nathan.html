Finished Typeface

Nathan Whitefield Typeface

I was focused on making a more playful typeface, intitially inspired by carnivals and elementary school bubble letters I moved on through different ideations until I created a form that I was happy with. I started skeching on Adobe XD using the circle tool primarily as I thought it would give me the best frame of referance for my final while also using rounded rectangles for this bubble letter effect I initially wanted. I wanted my typeface to coencide with my chosen poster theme, which was theorizing the web and with that in mind I made my letters be insie flashing rectangles with vertical lines. this was to resemble a computer screen. I wanted the letters to have a vintage but playful feel to them with the more curvy lettering and the horizontal repeat background.

Sketches

I started with creating itterations using Adobe XD. I created two, one was what I ended up basing my final typeface on while the latter was created to see how to make these letters on the grid and what ways I can configure a grid setup.
